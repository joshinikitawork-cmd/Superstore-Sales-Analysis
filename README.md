# Superstore Sales Analysis

## Business Problem
Identify which regions and product categories drive the most 
profit and where the business is losing margin.

## Dataset
Source: Kaggle, Sample Superstore Dataset
Size: 9,994 rows, 21 columns

## Tools Used
- Python: pandas, matplotlib, seaborn
- Power BI: Interactive dashboard with KPI cards, 
  bar charts, pie chart, and yearly sales trend

## Key Findings
- West region leads in sales ($725K) and profit ($108K)
- Furniture has a 2.5% profit margin despite $742K in sales
- Technology is the most profitable category at 17.4% margin
- Sales peak every November and December (seasonal pattern)
- Central region has critically low margins vs other regions

## Business Recommendations
1. Reduce discounts on Furniture in Central region
2. Push Technology products in South region
3. Plan Q4 inventory and marketing campaigns in advance
4. Investigate Central region margin underperformance
